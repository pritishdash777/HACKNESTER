// how it works

From solo to shipping in three steps

A simple flow to go from an idea or a skill set to a team that builds.

1

Build your profile

Add your skills, interests, and availability, then verify with your university email so people know you're a real student.

2

Post or discover a project

Share what you're building and the roles you need, or browse open projects that match the skills you want to grow.

3

Match and start building

Connect with the right collaborators, agree on roles, and get to work — with your progress showing up as proof of work.
